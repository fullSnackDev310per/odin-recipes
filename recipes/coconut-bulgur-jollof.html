Coconut Bulgur Jollof

Description

Coconut Bulgur Jollof is a spin on the classic jollof rice, a low carb option swapping rice with bulgur wheat and introducing the subtle flavour of coconut in the background. This classic west African dish is packed full of flavour with a rich tomato base soaked up by the toasted grains of the bulgur wheat.

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp Coconut Oil
  • Â½ Pound of Beef Fillet Diced
  • 1 Tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 Tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Small Ginger Grated
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic Minced
  • 1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Medium Onion Diced
  • 4 Medium Tomatoes Diced
  • 1 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1 Tbsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 2 Tsp Curry Powder
  • 2 Cups Bulgur Wheat
  • 300 ml Coconut Milk
  • 200 ml Water

Steps

  1. Heat the coconut oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the beef, oregano, black pepper, ginger, garlic, soy sauce, salt, and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the diced onion, stir until translucent, then add the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, stir and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the cayenne pepper, smoked paprika, curry powder, stir and let simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the bulgur wheat, braise for 5 minutes, then add salt, water, coconut milk, stir and let cook for 35-40 minutes.
  6. Bulgur wheat jollof ready to serve.
